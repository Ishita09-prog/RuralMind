# 🎬 RuralMind — 90-Second Demo Script

A tight walkthrough for a screen recording (use Loom, QuickTime, or any recorder).
Keep it under ~2 minutes. Have Ollama + backend + frontend running first.

---

### 0:00 — Hook (10s)
> "Millions of rural students fall behind for one reason — lessons aren't in their
> language. RuralMind fixes that. Watch."

Show the app open, clean chat screen.

### 0:10 — Multilingual core (25s)
1. Type **`barish kaise hoti hai?`** → answer comes back in Hinglish.
   > "I asked in Hinglish — it replied in Hinglish."
2. Type **`what is photosynthesis`** (pure English) → English answer.
   > "Switch to English, it follows."
3. Set the language dropdown to **Telugu**, ask anything → Telugu answer.
   > "Or force any language — here, Telugu — answer *and* voice."
4. Click the **▶ audio** to play the spoken answer.

### 0:35 — Learning tools (20s)
1. Click **Detailed** → fuller tutor-style explanation.
2. Click **Guide me** → it asks a leading question instead of answering.
3. Click **Diagram** → a real labeled science diagram loads.

### 0:55 — Beyond text (15s)
1. Click **📷**, upload a photo of a textbook question → OCR fills the box → Send.
   > "Can't type a long question? Snap a photo."
2. Click **Quiz** → an MCQ in the chosen language; pick the right answer → score goes up.

### 1:10 — Stickiness (15s)
1. Open the **Progress** tab → show streak, badges, topics studied.
2. Open the **Revise** tab → spaced-repetition review queue.
   > "Streaks, badges, and spaced revision keep kids coming back."

### 1:25 — Close (10s)
> "Multilingual, voice-first, works offline, and built for the students who need it
> most. That's RuralMind."

Show the logo / GitHub link.
